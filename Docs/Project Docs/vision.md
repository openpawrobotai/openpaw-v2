# OpenPaw — Vision

## The problem
Many people who want a pet can't watch over one. Dual-income households leave pets alone most of the day. Pets can't tell us when they feel unwell, and early symptoms are subtle — so owners notice too late, and routine issues become costly emergencies.

## The product
**OpenPaw** is a home robot companion for pets that:
- **Watches & interacts** — live video and play (laser pointer); check in from anywhere.
- **Senses** — temperature, motion, and periodic imaging of the pet (face, eyes, skin, coat).
- **Learns** — turns continuous observation into early health signals, grounded in veterinary knowledge.

## The moat
General models (e.g. Gemini) answer pet questions from text + a photo. They lack **real-time, longitudinal, breed-specific, environment-aware** data from inside the home:
- temperature trends, activity/movement patterns
- repeated coat/skin/eye imaging under consistent conditions
- correlated with breed, geography, ambient conditions

Across thousands of consented devices this becomes a dataset nobody else has — the foundation of **the world's first pet-health AI**. The device is open; the data + model are the business.

## Strategy: open device, private intelligence
- **Open:** firmware, electronics, mechanical, docs, website, marketing automation. Builds trust (a home camera demands it), community, and talent.
- **Private:** dataset + trained models (`openpaw-ai`), the app's backend surface, all credentials.

## Phases
1. **Device** — reliable robot: motion, sensors, video, OTA, app control.
2. **Data** — consented logging to build the dataset.
3. **Intelligence** — breed-aware health models surfacing early warnings.
4. **Platform** — partner with vets, groomers, pet businesses; expand species/breeds.

## Principles
- Privacy & consent first.
- Build in public; protect the data moat.
- Ground every health claim in veterinary knowledge.
